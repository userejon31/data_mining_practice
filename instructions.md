### Task 4. Integrative Case: Data Classification, Variables, Life Cycle, and Analytics

**Instructions**
Elaborate the analysis of the following case study, identifying data types, variable types, data life cycle phases, and analytics levels.

**Case: Café Aurora and its loyalty campaign**
Café Aurora is a local coffee shop that wants to increase its customers' repurchase rate. For a month, it launched a campaign called "Come back for your favorite coffee", aimed at people who had already purchased at least once in the branch or by home delivery. To evaluate the campaign, the coffee shop gathered information from different sources. From its sales system, it obtained the customer's name, city, purchase date, total amount paid, number of products purchased, and the purchase channel, which could be the branch, delivery app, or WhatsApp order. 

It also reviewed information from social networks. The marketing team analyzed Instagram comments, some Google Maps reviews, and several images posted by customers featuring the coffee shop's drinks. When organizing the information, the team found some problems. Some customers appeared duplicated, the dates did not always have the same format, and the purchase channel was written in different ways: "Whats", "WhatsApp", and "WA". Therefore, before analyzing the results, they corrected the records, unified the channel names, and separated the reviews into three satisfaction levels: low, medium, and high. 

After processing, Café Aurora identified that customers who purchased via WhatsApp had a higher repurchase rate, while customers arriving via delivery apps bought fewer products per order. It also observed that many negative reviews mentioned waiting times, while positive comments talked about the taste and presentation of the drinks. With these results, the coffee shop created a small dashboard with sales by channel, average purchase amount, satisfaction level, and repurchase frequency. Finally, it decided to strengthen WhatsApp customer service, improve delivery times, and offer a personalized promotion to frequent customers.
